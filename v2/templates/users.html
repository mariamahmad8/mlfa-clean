Role permissions

Admin
  • Full access to all inboxes, settings, and audit logs
  • Can create, edit, and delete inboxes
  • Can manage users and assign roles
  • Can toggle automation for any inbox
Owner
  • Access to the hub and settings for their assigned inboxes only
  • Can edit categories, recipients, templates for their inboxes
  • Can review and approve emails in the queue
  • Cannot manage users or add/delete inboxes
Reviewer
  • Can only approve or reject emails in the queue
  • Sees the hub for their assigned inboxes only
  • No settings access
