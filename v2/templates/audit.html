Audit log — what each action means
Auto
The AI handled the email on its own (classified, forwarded, replied, tagged) without human review.
Queued
The AI wasn't sure, so it added the email to the review hub for a person to look at.
Approved
A reviewer looked at the queued email and clicked Approve. The system then carried out the action plan.
Rejected
A reviewer rejected the queued email. It got moved to Trash and a note went out.
Dismissed
A reviewer decided the email needed no action (spam or irrelevant). Marked read, no reply, no forward.
Created / Updated / Deleted
A staff member added, edited, or removed something like a rule, template, recipient, or user.
